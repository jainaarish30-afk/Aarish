ABOUT THE EVENT

Pravah means "flow." It's a single day built to carry every kind of energy on campus — technical, creative, competitive — into one continuous current.

Open to students from all colleges, Pravah brings together coders, designers, performers, and thinkers for a single day of competition, collaboration, and celebration. Whether you build, perform, or compete — there's a stream here for you.

Expect a hackathon under lights, on-stage cultural battles, hands-on workshops from industry mentors, and a closing showcase where every track meets on one main stage.

FOUR TRACKS, ONE CURRENT

Find your stream

Every track runs in parallel through the day — pick one or hop between them.

💻

Tech Sprint

A 6-hour build hackathon, coding challenges, and a robotics showdown.

🎭

Culture Stage

Battle of Bands, dance crews, open mic, and a street-play face-off.

🧠

Mind Games

Quiz championships, debate rounds, and a case-study business sprint.

🛠️

Workshops

Hands-on sessions on AI, design, and product-building with mentors.
